Greenpoint Real Estate is a real estate company located in the San Francisco Bay Area. With multiple properties in their portfolio, they were in dire need of a property management system to help make their ever increasing number of tenants and partners more manageable. I was tasked with building 4 different websites that would service residents, property managers, admins, and investors for 21 different townhomes and apartments. To accomplish this, I used a combination of React, Node, and PostgreSQL to build, along with AWS S3, Amplify, and Heroku to test and deploy.
